FILM SYNOPSIS
A sketchy guardian (Rosamund Pike) who cons her elderly wards out of their savings meets her match in Netflix's I Care A Lot. All is going well for the con woman until she tries to pull the stunt on a woman (Dianne Weist) with powerful friends in a mafia-like crime organization. Pike won a Golden Globe for her performance in this twisted tale.
