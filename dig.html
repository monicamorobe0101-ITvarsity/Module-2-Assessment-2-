FILM SYNOPSIS
The Dig transports us to early 20th century Britain on the verge of World War II, where a wealthy widow (Carey Mulligan) enlists an archhaelogist to excavate the burial mounds on her land. What he unearths is a medieval ship, discovering all the secrets of history held within it.
